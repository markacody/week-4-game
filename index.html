Crystal Collector!

How to Play

A game starts automatically or when you click Restart. A new game starts when you win or lose.

  • At the start of each game, you are given a random Target Number between 19-120, Your Total Score is zero, and each crystal is assigned a randome value between 1 and 12.
  • The objective of the game is to match Your Total Score to the Target Number by "collecting" crystals.
  • Click a crystal (Ruby, Sapphire, Topaz, or Emerald) to add the value of the crystal to Your Total Score.
  • Pay attention! You won't know the value of the crystal until you click it the first time!
